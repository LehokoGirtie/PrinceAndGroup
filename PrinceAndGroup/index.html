About Us

Prince & group is a construction company offering services in general building and also civil engineering.

DATE OF CREATION:

Prince and Group Construction Services (K2020/145551/07) was established on 16 March 2020. This was a few weeks before the National Covid19 Lockdown. The enterprise was founded by Neo Prince Mphuti and the four (4) other co-owners. The company is registered for the purpose of rendering construction services and provide routine maintenance to clients. Our clients come in the form of government divisions and also extends to providing services to private entities as well.


MISSION:

To empower every person through a highly skilled team that emphasizes on reliability and developing long lasting relations with clients.


VISION:

To be the leading 100% youth owned enterprise that provides job opportunities for unemployed graduates while making an impact in every community around the country


OBJECTIVES:

Prince and Group Construction Services plans to:

  • Expand into Home Building and to be compliant with the safety and requirements of the CIDB.
  • Increase services and decrease costs by appointing permanent supervisors and also to add bookkeepers to the accounting staff.
  • Market other services of the business from year 4 in the operation of the business.
  • To secure at least 4 large scale contracts by the first 3 years of operation.
  • To offer graduates internship training at the company from the 4th year in operation.

    Safety Complience

    Legislative Framework
    The primary Acts that have an impact on Construction Health and Safety are the Occupational Health and Safety Act No. 85 of 1993 ( OH&S Act ). Then with it, the Compensation for Occupational Injuries and Diseases Act No. 130 of 1993 ( COID Act ) complements the Occupational Health and Safety Act.
    The company understands that Occupational Health and Safety is a fundamental human right of every worker is to be able to return home at the end of each working day; alive and healthy in the same physical condition that he/she commenced that working day.

    Prince and Group Construction Services acknowledges various legislation in the construction sector. Some of these regulations have direct or indirect links to Health and Safety, while other laws should make reference to Health and Safety:
    • Basic Conditions of Employment Act No. 75 of 1997 as amended by the Basic Conditions of Employment Act No. 11 of 2002: “The intention of the act is to give effect to the right to fair labour practices”
    • Labour Relations Act No. 66 of 1995 as Amended: This Act recognizes the right of every worker to fair labour practices as Stated in Section 14 of the Constitution and Participation in Decision-making in the workplace.
    • National Building Regulations and Standards Act No. 103 of 1977
    This Act addresses Health and Safety issues to both the public and workforce.
